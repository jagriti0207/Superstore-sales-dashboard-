# SuperStore Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project is based on the analysis of SuperStore sales data using Power BI. The main goal of this project is to understand sales performance, customer behavior, shipping modes, and product categories. A 15-day sales forecast is also included to help businesses with short-term planning and decision-making.

---

## Objective
- Analyze overall sales, profit, and quantity  
- Understand customer segments and their contribution  
- Identify popular shipping and payment modes  
- Track sales and profit trends over time  
- Forecast sales for the next 15 days  

---

## Dashboard Summary

### Key Metrics
- Total Sales: 341K  
- Total Profit: 27K  
- Total Quantity Sold: 5239  
- Profit Ratio: 4%  

---

### Sales by Customer Segment
The Consumer segment contributes the highest sales, followed by Corporate and Home Office customers.

---

### Payment Mode Analysis
Most customers prefer Cash on Delivery (COD) and Online payments, while Card payments are used less frequently.

---

### Year-wise Sales & Profit Trend
Sales and profit show an overall positive trend when comparing 2019 and 2020, indicating business growth.

---

### Shipping Mode Analysis
Standard Class is the most used shipping mode, while Same Day delivery is used the least.

---

### Category & Sub-Category Analysis
- Office Supplies generate the highest sales among all categories  
- Furniture and Technology also contribute significantly  
- Chairs, Binders, and Phones are top-performing sub-categories  

---

### State-wise Sales
California records the highest sales, followed by New York and Texas.

---

## 15-Day Sales Forecast
The dashboard includes a 15-day sales forecast based on historical data. The forecast helps in understanding future demand patterns and supports inventory and sales planning.

---

## Key Insights
- Consumer segment is the main revenue driver  
- COD and Online payments dominate transactions  
- Standard Class shipping is most preferred  
- Office Supplies lead in overall sales  
- Sales show steady growth over time  

---

## Business Use Cases
- Helps businesses plan inventory using sales forecasts  
- Supports better marketing strategies based on customer segments  
- Improves decision-making for shipping and payment options  
- Useful for tracking category-wise and state-wise performance
